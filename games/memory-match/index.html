Memory Match: How to Play

Welcome to **Memory Match**, a classic card game that tests your memory and rewards you for every match! The goal is to find all the matching pairs of cards on the board.

How to Play

1. Flip Cards: Click or tap on any two cards to flip them over. You can only flip two cards at a time.

2. Make a Match: If the two cards you flip have the same icon, it's a match! The cards will remain face-up. You'll also earn points for each successful match.

3. Keep Trying: If the cards don't match, they will automatically flip back over. Use your memory to remember where each card is located.

Earning Points

Each time you find a successful match, you earn **100 points**. When you clear the entire board by finding all the pairs, you will get a bonus of **500 points**. These points are automatically added to your account!
