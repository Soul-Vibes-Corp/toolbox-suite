Candy Match: How to Play

Welcome to **Candy Match**, a classic puzzle game where you can earn points for your account! The goal is to match three or more identical candies in a row or column to clear them and earn points. You have 60 seconds to get the highest score possible.

How to Play

1. Swap Candies: Click or tap a candy and then click or tap an adjacent candy to swap their positions.

2. Match Three or More: If swapping creates a line of three or more identical candies, they will be cleared from the board. New candies will fall from the top to fill the empty spaces. .

3. Beat the Clock: You have 60 seconds to earn as many points as possible. The game ends when the timer runs out.

Earning Points

Every time you make a successful match, your score will increase. Your total score at the end of the 60-second round will be automatically added to your account and reflected on your dashboard.
