Word Guess: How to Play

Welcome to **Word Guess**, a daily puzzle that lets you earn points for your account!

The Goal

Your objective is to guess a hidden 5-letter word in six attempts. You win by guessing the correct word.

How to Play

1. Type any 5-letter word using the on-screen keyboard or your device's physical keyboard and press "Enter".

2. After each guess, the tiles will change color to give you feedback on your letters:

  • **Green:** A letter is in the correct spot.
  • **Yellow:** A letter is in the word, but it's in the wrong spot.
  • **Gray:** The letter is not in the word at all.

Earning Points

If you successfully guess the word, you will automatically be awarded **250 points** for your account. The points are added instantly to your total and will be reflected on your dashboard and rewards page.
