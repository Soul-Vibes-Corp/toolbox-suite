Blackjack: How to Play

Welcome to **Blackjack**, a classic card game where you play against the dealer! The goal is to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over. You start with **10,000 points** to play with.

How to Play

1. Place Your Bet: Before the game starts, select your bet amount. Each game costs this amount, which is deducted from your points.

2. Get Your Cards: Click "Deal" to start the game. You and the dealer will each receive two cards. One of the dealer's cards will be hidden.

3. Hit or Stand: Your turn begins. You can "Hit" to take another card or "Stand" to keep your current hand. Be careful not to go over 21!

Scoring

  • Numbered cards are worth their face value.
  • J, Q, and K are all worth 10.
  • Aces are worth either 1 or 11.

Winning and Losing

If your final score is higher than the dealer's without going over 21, you win double your bet. If you get a Blackjack (21 on your first two cards), you win 1.5 times your bet. If you and the dealer tie, it's a "push," and you get your bet back.
