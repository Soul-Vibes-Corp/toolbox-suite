Casino Slots: How to Play

Welcome to the Casino Slots, where you can wager your points to win big! The goal is to get matching icons on the reels. You start with **10,000 points** to play.

How to Play

1. Select Your Bet: Choose your bet amount (100, 500, or 1000 points) before you spin. The cost of each spin is deducted from your total points.

2. Pull the Handle: Click the "Spin" button to start the reels. They will spin and stop one by one, revealing your fate!

Earning Points

If you get matching icons, you win! The larger the bet, the bigger the potential payout.

  • Triple Sevens: Win 20,000 points (Jackpot!)
  • Triple Stars: Win 10,000 points
  • Triple Bells: Win 5,000 points
  • Any three matching icons: Win 2,500 points

Your point total is automatically updated in real-time. Good luck!
